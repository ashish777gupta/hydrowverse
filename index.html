A Water Intelligence Platform
About

Hydrow Technologies is a water intelligence platform providing solutions for monitoring water in realtime for tanks, fluid flow in pipes and detecting leakages.

  • Realtime updates of the water levels and flow can be viewed and alerts of possible leakages are given.
  • Water wastage and losses in the form of NRW (Non Revenue Water) can be monitored and mitigated.
  • Providing end to end monitoring solutions on water management for your industry and campus.
